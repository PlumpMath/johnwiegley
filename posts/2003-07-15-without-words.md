---
title: Without words
date: Tue, 15 Jul 2003 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

Edited: 04/10/2004
Location: Italy

    It can all be said  in one word  if our hearts share  the  
    vocabulary.

    But even then,  
    it's not to be said --  
    better if shown --  
    the nights themselves  
    will tell the meaning.
