title: Slow
date: Fri, 15 Jul 2016 15:52:10 +0000
category: Poems
---

    In a time languorous
    the moments are honey
    long, drawn-out seconds
    pool around me
    a slow, slow heat
    pervades my being.
    Yesterday, my other life.
    is lost to memory.
    The future calls in
    mellifluous tones
    falling, falling
    a thousand years from now.
