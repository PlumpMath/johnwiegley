---
title: An Iranian beauty
date: Tue, 05 Apr 2005 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

Edited: 05/04/2005
Location: 7373 N. 71st Place, Paradise Valley, AZ

    Her hair falls  
    like ebon silk  
    flowing to her sides;

    Her fair skin  
    a honeyed milk  
    that calms my thirsty eyes;

    Her sweet smile  
    undoes my heart,  
    time dallies in that stare;

    I wonder still  
    those cherry lips...  
    Let every man beware.
