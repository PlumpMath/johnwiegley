title: Temple of Pain
date: Tue, 15 Jul 2003 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

Edited: 03/22/2004
Location: Italy

    A gym  
    is a Temple of Pain  
    to which  
    the votaries of Will retire  
    and heap their bodies  
    on pyres of agony  
    burning away  
    the minutes and the hours  
    of their devotion  
    until that exhaustion  
    which is their spirit's joy  
    is returned.
