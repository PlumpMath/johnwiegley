---
title: Consumed
date: Mon, 28 Mar 2005 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

Edited: 04/19/2005
Location: Tempe Bahá'í Center, Arizona

    O fire, beautiful fire!  
    Your pain so intense  
    it tickles my soul.

    I am burnt today,  
    my skin is black and  
    puffed and ruined.

    Little by little  
    you turn me into  
    a delicate smoke;

    Up, up, into the vast sky I go  
    floating on the finest breezes.
