---
title: Falling rain
date: Sat, 23 Oct 2004 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

Location: 27342 Via Burgos, Mission Viejo, CA

    the drops of the rain  
    fall steadily down;  
    the grey clouds  
    the muted breeze  
    the scents  
    of a sodden earth.  
    the water soaks  
    into the ground's  
    own marrow.  
    the world is slowly  
    being dissolved  
    by a cleansing sky.
