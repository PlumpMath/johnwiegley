---
title: The Arno in the late sun
date: Tue, 15 Jul 2003 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

Edited: 04/10/2004
Location: Italy

    The Arno in the late sun:  
    speckles of gold and silver  
    on an olive green;  
    green and gold and silver...  
    a treasure of coins  
    all minted in light  
    on a moving, velvet green...
