---
title: Silence
date: Wed, 29 Oct 2003 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

    Silence

    allows me to think,  
      to feel,  
      to dream,  
      to wonder,  
      to realize,  
      to awaken,  
      to know.

    I watch your eyes  
    play silently in the light --  
    what else is there?
