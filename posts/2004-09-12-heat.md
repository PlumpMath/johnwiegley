title: Heat
date: Sun, 12 Sep 2004 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

Location: 380 Esplanade Ave #211

    Whenever I see you,  
    my eyes feel as if  
    soaked in wine  
    and kindled into flame...

    Two burning orbs,  
    which lose their color  
    in the ruddy fire.  
    All vision is gone  
    but your memory.
