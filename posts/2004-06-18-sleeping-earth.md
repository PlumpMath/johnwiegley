---
title: Sleeping earth
date: Fri, 18 Jun 2004 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

Location: 380 Esplanade Ave #211

    Tonight, only peace.  
    The stars  
    wheel about  
    their constellations...  
    the sea  
    recounts  
    a hushed lullaby...  
    the moon  
    winks  
    at a sleeping earth...  
    and a herd of  
    silvered clouds  
    pass by.
