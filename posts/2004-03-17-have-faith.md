---

title: Have faith
date: Wed, 17 Mar 2004 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

    Great is the mountain,  
    and vast is the sea;  
    and deeper the mystery,  
    the farther I see.

    Never wonder the beauty,  
    Never question the love;  
    if peace is not possible,  
    Who gave us the dove?
