title: Le morte
date: Wed, 17 Mar 2004 12:00:00 +0000
category: Poems
---

    When that moment comes,  
    and I hear the candle's light  
      go out in a whisper,  
    where will I be then?

    They call it death here;  
    but I wonder  
    whether what I've seen so far  
    you would call life.
